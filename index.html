Conference Topics

ISEE 2026 at RUET highlights cutting-edge research in energy and environmental fields, fostering sustainable technologies and solutions for global challenges.

  • Energy

  • Environment

  • Sustainability

Advanced Energy Systems

Explores innovative energy technologies, efficiency improvements, and next-gen solutions for cleaner and smarter power systems.

  • Renewable Energy Technologies
  • Hydrogen and Fuel Cell Technologies
  • Nuclear Energy & Safety
  • Green & Advanced Energy Materials
  • Energy Systems Modeling & Digital Transformation
  • Energy Efficiency, Conservation & Storage

Environmental Challenges & Solutions

Focuses on climate resilience, pollution control, and sustainable practices to protect ecosystems and human health.

  • Climate Change Mitigation & Adaptation
  • Carbon Capture, Utilization & Storage (CCUS)
  • Pollution Control & Remediation
  • Circular Economy & Waste Management
  • Marine, Coastal & Agricultural Sustainability
  • Environmental Health & Risk Assessment
  • Life-Cycle Assessment & Impact Analysis

Sustainable Development & Infrastructure

Addresses urban growth, smart cities, and sustainable transport to build resilient and future-ready infrastructure.

  • Sustainable Transportation & Fuels
  • Sustainable Building & Urban Infrastructure
  • Smart Cities & Climate-Resilient Infrastructure
  • Environmental Policy, Governance & Economics
