Keynote Speakers

The keynote sessions at ISEE 2024 will feature distinguished experts and thought leaders from academia and industry. They will share insights on emerging trends, cutting-edge research, and future challenges in Mechanical, Industrial, and Sustainable Engineering, inspiring innovation and collaboration among participants.

Professor Dr. Biddyut Paul

RMIT University, Australia

Focused on advancing clean and sustainable energy technologies, with expertise in PEM fuel cells, electrolysers, and unitised regenerative fuel cells (URFCs). Research also covers photovoltaics, solar thermal systems, and energy efficiency, aiming to accelerate the global transition to renewable energy.

Professor Dr. Md. Nurul Islam

Rajshahi University of Engineering & Technology, Bangladesh

Specialized in solid oxide fuel cell (SOFC) materials for clean energy generation, with additional expertise in solar cell material simulation and fabrication. Research interests also include composite materials for automotive and high-temperature applications, as well as ultrasonic evaluation techniques using SAM, SEM, AFM, and advanced optical microscopy.

Dr. Mahadi Hasan Masud

Rajshahi University of Engineering & Technology, Bangladesh

Focused on bio-inspired vehicle design, with expertise in aerodynamics and hydrodynamics. Research work emphasizes the application of computational fluid dynamics (CFD) to develop innovative, efficient, and sustainable engineering solutions.

Dr. Monjur Mourshed

Rajshahi University of Engineering & Technology, Bangladesh

Specializes in hydrogen energy technologies, including fuel cells, electrolysers, and hydrogen storage. Research also extends to flow batteries, renewable energy storage and management, and innovative solutions in waste-to-energy conversion for sustainable power systems.
